Sweet Potatoes

  • The daily requirement is 130g.

  • The chemical yield is 1500g/m2.

  • The organic yield is 1200g/m2.

  • The land requirement is 40m2.

Carrots

  • The daily requirement is 120g fresh or 240g when drying.

  • The chemical yield is 4428g/m2.

  • The organic yield is 3542g/m2.

  • The land requirement is 25m2.
